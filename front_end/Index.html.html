How It Works

Each item gets a unique QR code or serial number for easy identification and tracking. Simply scan to view item details.

Items are automatically tagged with GPS coordinates when reported. See exactly where items were lost or found.

Real-time Alerts

Get instant notifications when items matching your description are found nearby or when your lost item is scanned.
